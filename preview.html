Rather than a traditional trailer, we present a 20-minute Proof of Concept Assembly, drawn from more than fifteen hours of newly recorded interviews, archival research and location photography.

This Cinematic Assembly is designed to demonstrate the film's narrative structure, emotional scope, visual approach, and central themes. It introduces principal characters, historical events and contemporary voices that will shape the completed documentary while allowing viewers to experience the story's emotional and thematic progression.

The assembly should be viewed not as a promotional trailer, but as an early framework of a feature documentary film. 

The completed film will be presented to major film festivals, streamers and many educational partners.
